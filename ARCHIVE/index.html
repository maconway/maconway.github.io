Mike Conway is a senior lecturer in Digital Health at the University of Melbourne's School of Computing & Information Systems with research interests focused on the application of computational methods to address public health problems, particularly in the broad areas of mental health and substance use.

Prior to joining the University of Melbourne in 2021, Conway held research positions at the University of Pittsburgh, Mayo Clinic, the University of California San Diego, and was for several years a tenure-track assistant professor at the University of Utah's Department of Biomedical Informatics.
